OHM

Just like in the prominent neighboring club (Tresor) this club is located in the same building as the club Tresor but it has a different entrance, there is electronic music to be heard in OHM, especially techno and house, but also more off the beaten track. The club has a pleasantly clear size and is valued by connoisseurs of the scene. One of the most known parties there is called Mess and it hosts female djs only, giving space to womens in such a male dominant industry. The club's program managers are also open to art and so there are regular parties that combine music and installations or performances.
