EAT & DRINK

Instead of settling for another crappy currywurst, this guide shows you where to eat and drink in Berlin's main neighborhoods..

Pizza Peppino

Stop here for late night pizzas.The place serves authentic Italian food @ reasonable rates.Above all the staffs here speak English and are extremely friendly..

Jim Block

This place serves one of the best burgers in town.Great food with nice and cozy atmosphere and is a great go-to place for people visiting Berlin...

Salamat

This place has a quite and peaceful atmosphere & the staffs are also friendly.They serve excellent Arabic food with exotic flavors and wide varieties..

Lia's Kitchen

If you are a vegan and a burger lover, this place is a must visit. This place serves only Vegan food which is damn delicious.Highly Recommended..

Badfish Bar

This is one superb looking bar,a great interior, exposed brickwork, candle lit and cool looking serving bar. There are also plenty of seating options spread throughout this bar both inside and outside .
